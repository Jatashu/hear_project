MY MODE: As individual as you are.

4 exclusive functions that make listening perfect.

Everyone lives, feels, and hears differently, and yet almost all hearing aids are the same. Until now! With the hear.com HORIZON smartphone app, you can quickly and easily adapt your hearing aid to your individual hearing situation with just a tap! Doesn’t matter if you’re playing a sport, are at a concert, or relaxing on the couch, the exclusive MY MODE function makes your HORIZON adapt to your life.

  • Features designed exclusively for HORIZON
  • Activate with just a tap of your finger via the app
  • The perfect mode for every situation

Try it yourself:

MUSIC MODE

MUSIC MODE

Enjoy the soundtrack of life.

Turn on effect

From Rock to Classical, streaming or live in concert – with MUSIC MODE, your music sounds the way it should. Normally, your hearing aid processes all audio signals and optimizes them for better speech understanding, MUSIC MODE ensures a more symphonic sound experience: Loud sounds loud, quiet stays quiet and all the subtleties that make your favourite songs great are clearly audible!

ACTIVE MODE

ACTIVE MODE

Keeps up with your life.
Play
Turn on effect

Your HORIZON device is there for you in leading an active life. Head out for a bike ride, a long walk on the beach, a tennis match, or any other physical activity with your hearing aids on! With ACTIVE MODE, wind noise is additionally suppressed for good speech understanding in these situations. Your device is tuned to process rapid changes in direction so you can hear better while active.

QUIET MODE

QUIET MODE

Quiet can sound so good.
Play
Turn on effect

Although it’s not the typical expectation from a hearing aid, but your HORIZON device can also ensure that you hear almost nothing. In QUIET MODE, all ambient noise is greatly reduced and sudden sounds are suppressed. Much like a library, there is a pleasing silence and you can relax and concentrate on what’s important. Because as nice as it is to understand everything, it can also be nice not hear anything.

RELAX MODE

RELAX MODE

The integrated wellness program.
Play
Turn on effect

Embrace deep relaxation at the push of a button. With RELAX MODE, ambient noise is faded out and relaxing sounds are activated in a frequency mix scientifically proven to help calm the mind. Allow your thoughts to rest and elevate your holistic well-being. Stress-relieving and concentration-enhancing: you can switch off easily and effectively with RELAX MODE.

SPEECH FOCUS

SPEECH FOCUS

Crystal clear speech understanding - anytime, anywhere.

Turn on effect

Full focus on what is really important – even in difficult listening environments crowded restaurants or conference rooms. With SPEECH FOCUS you activate an extra boost of particularly accentuated speech understanding in milliseconds. All speech-relevant frequencies are highlighted and disturbing ambient noises are filtered out.

So you can follow your conversation with your partners again with absolute ease.

  • Understand clearly
  • Automatically dim ambient noise
  • Optimal speech understanding, even in noisy surroundings
